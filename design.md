Onboarding (Orientation)
Goal: Collect only the essentials.
Required fields:
Name
Age
Sex
Orientation
Notes:
Everything beyond these four items is intro UI, not data collection.
User should feel oriented, not interrogated.
App Pages Overview (4 Tabs)
1. Town Hall
Purpose: User feedback + bug reporting.
Design Notes:
Non-social page.
Simple forms and status messages only.
Avoid mixing with social content.
2. Map
Purpose: Let users choose their preferred “spot.”
Important:
No location permissions required.
Users manually select a location.
Each map marker = a Bar.
Design Notes:
Treat markers as entry points, not GPS-dependent objects.
Map is purely navigational and user-guided.
3. Bar
Purpose: The place where people appear.
Definition:
Every map marker has its own Bar (a pool of users).
User sets one as their "home bar" (name not final).
Design Notes:
Think of Bars as small social clusters.
Bar selection defines who the user sees in discovery.
4. Table (Modern replacement for “Matches”)
Purpose: Hub for interactions + self-management.
Contains three modules:
Your Matches — matches and invitations you've sent.
Invitations to You — people who invited you.
Your Profile & Preferences — view/edit profile, preferences, settings.
Design Notes:
Treat this as a dashboard.
Clear segmentation between the three modules.
Users should understand where each relationship lives.
